# Pivot Decision Record

**D-ESS MVP = Neuss Area Opportunity Radar for area-level opportunity prioritization. It does not perform household-level confirmation or operational activation.**

## Frozen Baseline
The legacy governance track is officially frozen at **Stage 76: Remediation Intake & Revalidation Loop**.

## Pivot Rationale
The D-ESS engine successfully built an impenetrable, audit-proof pipeline handling GDPR consent, verification, commercial readiness, and state-transition execution locks. However, to immediately deliver tangible business value and visualize the "where to attack first" strategy, the project must pivot from deep household-level legal architecture to macro area-level opportunity scoring.

## Old Direction Summary
A highly rigorous, pessimistic machine focused on transforming individual leads into legally and commercially actionable CRM tasks through 76 distinct stages of governance gates, execution simulations, and evidence intaking.

## New Direction Summary
"Neuss Area Opportunity Radar MVP" – A lightweight, pragmatic spatial ranking tool that aggregates existing signals (roof PV potential, building mix, proxies) at the area level to highlight the most lucrative district clusters for targeted action, accompanied by business-readable sales explanations.

## What is Excluded from MVP Runtime
- Household confirmation mapping
- Contact discovery algorithms
- CRM pushing or task creation
- GDPR Consent chaining and legal basis review
- Remediation intake and retry authorization
- Any activation execution permissioning

## Role of Stage 76 After Pivot
Stage 76 serves as the definitive structural baseline for enterprise/commercial-grade compliance. It is not abandoned; it is retained as the "frozen" module for future workflows where actual B2C execution relies on 100% audit-proof lineage.
