# Stage 2 Source Selection Report (Neuss Pilot)

## Overview
This report documents the selection of real official sources for the Field 03B audit of the Neuss-Allerheiligen pilot area. The selection focuses on "Golden Sources" from the City of Neuss and Stadtwerke Neuss (swn).

## Selected Sources

### 1. Neuss Municipal Heat Plan (KWP) Summary
- **Source ID**: `SRC_NEUSS_KWP_2025_SUMMARY`
- **Type**: Official PDF Report
- **Coverage**: City-level
- **Decision Eligible**: YES
- **Why Selected**: This is the sovereign legal basis for heat planning in Neuss. Crucially, it confirms that no mandatory connection bylaw (Anschluss- und Benutzungszwang) is applied to any zone in Neuss as of July 2025. This allows HPs in all areas provided they meet national GEG requirements.

### 2. swn Allerheiligen FFVAV Technical Data
- **Source ID**: `SRC_SWN_ALLERHEILIGEN_FFVAV_2025`
- **Type**: Technical PDF
- **Coverage**: District-level (Allerheiligen)
- **Decision Eligible**: YES
- **Why Selected**: Confirms the existence of the district heating network in the pilot area. Provides high-resolution technical proof that the network is operational and uses industrial waste heat (Alunorf).

### 3. swn Pricing Leaflet (Oct 2025)
- **Source ID**: `SRC_SWN_PRICING_2025_OCT`
- **Type**: Pricing Doc
- **Coverage**: District-level (Allerheiligen)
- **Decision Eligible**: NO (Context Only)
- **Why Selected**: Validates that district heating is actively sold and supported in the specific neighborhood. Useful for confirming current network availability.

### 4. Neuss Heat Transition Strategy
- **Source ID**: `SRC_NEUSS_KWP_STRATEGY`
- **Type**: Strategic PDF
- **Coverage**: City-level
- **Decision Eligible**: YES
- **Why Selected**: Provides the long-term roadmap. Useful for identifying planned expansion zones beyond the existing network.

## Relationship to Field 02 Pilot
- **Geographic Match**: All sources cover the `ALLERHEILIGEN_PILOT_SEG_01` area either directly (swn docs) or as part of the total city coverage.
- **Evidence Gap**: While a zoning map is mentioned in search, a high-resolution segment-by-segment map was not "downloaded" in this phase; the KWP text claims will serve as primary evidence.
