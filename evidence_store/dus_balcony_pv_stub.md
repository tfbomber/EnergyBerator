# Richtlinie zur Förderung von Balkonkraftwerken (Klimapakt Düsseldorf)
Version: 2026-V1
Source: https://www.duesseldorf.de/klimapakt

## Section 2.1: Eligible Costs
Costs for the purchase of the photovoltaic system (module, inverter, mounting material) are eligible. 
Installation costs by specialized companies are also eligible.
VAT is handled according to JStG 2022 (0% for hardware).

## Section 4.2: Start of Measures (Vorhabenbeginn)
The application must be submitted **before** the start of the measure.
Start of measure is defined as:
- Placing a binding order (Contract Signed)
- Start of installation (Work Started)
- Making any payment (Payment Made)

Planning and consultation services are not considered a start of measure.

## Section 5.1: Amount of Funding
The funding amounts to 50% of the eligible costs, up to a maximum of **600 EUR** per system.
